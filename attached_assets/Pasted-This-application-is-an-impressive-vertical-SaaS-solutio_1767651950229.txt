#### B. Mobile "Field Mode" UI

Your report notes that the Kanban is horizontal and optimized for desktop.

* **Optimization:** Field technicians are your primary data source. If they cannot easily tap "Mark Scanning Complete" on a phone with gloves on or in bright sunlight, they won't use it.
* **Target:** Implement a **bottom-sheet navigation** or a simplified **List View** for screens under 768px specifically for the `/production` and `/tools` routes.

#### C. Data Normalization (Zod/Drizzle Schema)

Currently, "Building Type" and "Scope" appear to be open text.

Based on the **Application Status Report** you provided and our previous strategic roadmap, here is a professional review of the current **Scan2Plan OS** build.

### **Functional Review: Scan2Plan OS v1.0.0**

The current build is a solid technical foundation. It establishes the "Command Center" aesthetic and core CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) capabilities. However, when measured against our goal of a **"Project Lifecycle Bridge,"** there are several critical gaps that need to be addressed to make this a truly proactive tool for a remote CEO.

---

### **1. The Wins: What’s Working Well**

* **The "Command Center" UI:** The use of Shadcn/ui and Tailwind with a dark theme/glassmorphism perfectly matches the "High-Tech CEO" vibe we discussed.
* **Basic AI Utility:** The "Field Notes AI Tool" successfully demonstrates the value of LLMs (GPT-4o-mini) in turning unstructured technician "brain dumps" into professional scopes.
* **Staleness Awareness:** The dashboard correctly identifies "Action Items" for leads over 14 days old. This is the first step toward the **Automated Probability Engine** we planned.

---

### **2. Critical Gaps: Vision vs. Reality**

To move this from a "Standard CRM" to the "Scan2Plan OS," we need to address these three areas:

#### **A. AI Intelligence: From Text to Media**

* **Current State:** The app processes *text* notes into scopes.
* **The Gap:** In our strategy, we agreed the technician records **video walkthroughs with voice-overs**. The app currently lacks an **Audio/Video processing pipeline**.
* **Recommendation:** Integrate the **OpenAI Whisper API**. Instead of a text box, the "Tools" page needs a file upload for the tech’s audio/video. The AI should then transcribe the audio and perform the "Technical Extraction" (Ceiling heights, obstacles, etc.) as outlined in the PRD.

#### **B. The "Bridge": Automation vs. Manual Entry**

* **Current State:** QuickBooks and Airtable integrations are listed as "Limitations" or placeholders.
* **The Gap:** The CEO’s primary "Time Reclaimed" comes from **One-Click Handoffs**. Without the QuickBooks SKU mapping and Airtable project creation, the CEO still has to do "Double Entry."
* **Recommendation:** Prioritize the **Server-Side Integration**. The `server/routes.ts` needs endpoints that take a `Closed Won` lead and use the Airtable/QuickBooks APIs to create records automatically.

#### **C. Project Economics: The Missing "Actuals"**

* **Current State:** Dashboard shows total pipeline value, but no "Economic Drill-down."
* **The Gap:** You have the Airtable schema for `Time_Entries` and labor costs. To achieve **Granular Economics**, the app must fetch those entries and compare them to the `value` field in the `leads` table.
* **Recommendation:** Add a `profit_analysis` route that joins your `projects` table with an external fetch from Airtable.
